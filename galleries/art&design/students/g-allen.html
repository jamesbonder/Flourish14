Gemma Allen

BA (Hons) Interior Design


About Gemma's Work

I will be graduating from the BA Interior Design course at the University of South Wales in July and I am looking forward to taking the next steps towards starting a career in Interior Design. Throughout my time at university, I have learnt a great deal about the subject and have enjoyed every minute of developing skills to be used in the Interior Design industry.

I am confident in using AutoCAD and 3d Studio Max to create plans, sections, elevations and visuals to communicate my designs effectively. I am also confident in using Photoshop to add to these drawings and to improve my presentation. I have a keen eye for detail and would love an opportunity to get into the industry to develop the skills I have.
