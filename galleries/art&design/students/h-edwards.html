BA (Hons) Graphic Communication


About Hollie's Work

I am a Graphic Communication student with a real passion and drive for design. I have an interest in a wide variety of graphic design and enjoy both digital and print based design.

I also love experimenting with different materials, especially lino cutting and often carry a notebook around with me to fill with sketches and doodles when inspiration strikes.

During my time at university I have completed a number of projects and brought different styles and ideas to each of them. I am eager to get started in the industry of graphic design and look forward to the new experiences and challenges I shall face and overcome over the years.
