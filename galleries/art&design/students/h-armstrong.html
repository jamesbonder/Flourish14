Hayley Armstrong

BA (Hons) Advertising Design


About Hayley's Work

I am a creative and organised individual based in South Wales with a multi-disciplinary spirit and a strong passion for advertising. I enjoy each and every aspect from initial development to pitching to clients. As well as advertising I enjoy traveling, rock climbing and cheerleading in my spare time.

During my studies at USW I undertook work placements within graphic design, social media, advertising and print production in order to gain experience and knowledge of the different areas within the industry. I have worked on many live briefs for many companies such as Fallon London, Ethos Creative, Boulders Climbing Centre and 3VTV. I have also completed work placements at Grey London, Rizon Studios, South Wales Argus Newspaper, Ozum and Dischromedia.
