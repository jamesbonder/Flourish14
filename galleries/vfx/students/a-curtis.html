About Adam's Work

Hello! I'm Adam Curtis, a designer who for the past 3 years has been studying game design in the University of South Wales.

Since using the Adobe Creative Suite for several years I have gained an interest in graphic design and 2D animation, but have also dabbled in film with a keen eye for cinematography. With these tools at my disposal I developed two narrative experiences questioning what the video game medium can achieve in terms of delivering a story to the player over the course of the 3 year period. I also began development of a unique perspective-based environmental puzzle game never seen before in the industry, and I hope to begin full production in the foreseeable future

At this year's Flourish 2014 show I will be presenting Fragments, a game that takes the industry classic formula of a hero and guide traversing a vast landscape shrouded in mystery, but flips it on its head, with the twist being the player plays as the guide instead.

You can find me on Twitter where I'll be updating frequently over the summer the development of Ally, a game chosen to take part in Dare To Be Digital 2014 in the University of Abertay.
