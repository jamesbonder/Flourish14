Martin Giles

BA (Hons) Game Art


About Martin's Work

Hi my name is Martin Giles and I am a environment and technical artist, In my final year of University studying Game Art & Animation at the University Of South Wales.

I specialise in the creation of both hard surface and organic environments but also have the technical understanding of shaders and lighting techniques in games engines such as Unity and CryEngine. I Primarily use Autodesk 3DS Max, Zbrush, Xnormals, PhotoShop, CryEngine. I also have experience with Autodesk Maya and PhotoShop Plugins DDO and NDO.

View more of Martin's work at his website.
