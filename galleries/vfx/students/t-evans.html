About Tomos's Work

Hello! I'm Tomos Hywel Evans, a designer who for the past 3 years has been studying game design in the University of South Wales.

I'm a competitive and passionate gamer with a strong desire to go into the Games Industry as an artist. I'm dedicated to my work, trying to produce high quality work, using complex 3D modeling and sculpting softwares. My strongest interest is towards producing creatures, characters and environments that run smoothly within a Games Engine. I spend most of my time learning from tutorial videos on Youtube and Digita Tutors, aiming to achieve industry standard skills within the coming year.

View more of Tomos' work at his website.
