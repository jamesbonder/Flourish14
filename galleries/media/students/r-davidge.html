About Ria's Work

My name is Robyn Davidge and I have studied Media Production at the University of South Wales. My aspiration is to pursue a career in the film industry and I have worked hard throughout my degree to make this happen. Alongside film, I also have strong interests in fashion photography and graphic design, which I shall continue to carry out as potential freelance work or simply as a hobby.

On my recent productions, I have performed the Director role and concluded that this is where I feel most comfortable. I have also exercised the roles of Scriptwriter, Producer, Camera Operator, Sound Recorder and Editor, which gives me a well rounded view of the filmmaking process.

My ambition is begin working as a runner, or perhaps even an Assistant Director if I am to secure work on a smaller production. Either way, I am confident that my enthusiasm and eagerness to work in this industry will see me make the most of every opportunity that is to arise.
