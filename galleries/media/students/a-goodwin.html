Holly Gardner

BA (Hons) Media Technology


About Holly's Work

Media Technology is a course that has unlimited opportunities and outcomes. For me the course has been a launchpad for roads into the world of radio, web and television.

During my final year I embarked upon the most exciting project so far, Exposure TV. I supported the University of South Wales TV channel's output with motion graphics and on screen branding. With the title of broadcast designer I supported producers and directors by responding to their visual needs to give programmes a polished and continuous layer of branding whether it be an opening title sequence, an ident, the straps for presenters and guests names, or a viewer's tweet scrolling along the bottom of the screen, both of which would be composited, rendered and played out live by myself.

The project began with of months of pilots leading up to 6 broadcasting days featuring over 15 live shows, including news bulletins and magazine programmes in the style of The One Show or This Morning.

Thanks to this experience I have some serious passion and ambition to want to work in live television production, having embarked upon work experience at places such as BBC Three previously.
