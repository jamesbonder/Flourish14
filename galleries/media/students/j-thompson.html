Jack Thompson

BA (Hons) Media Production


About Jack's Work

In my three years studying Media Production at the Atrium, I have gained skills and experience in many areas of the media; though I have specialised in field of filmmaking. I have worked on a number of short film projects as Director of Photography, both in and outside of the curriculum, where I have had the chance to create and tell stories through lighting styles and camera techniques; further developing my skills as a Cinematographer. This includes my final year short film project Rank, which I worked on as Director of Photography, Camera Operator and Colourist.

After leaving university I hope to pursue a career in the film industry by working my way up through the camera and lighting departments with the aim ultimately of becoming a Director of Photography at a professional level.

Other areas I have studied and have interest in are Documentary Photography and Interactive Media. In my second year I produced a 50 image photography book titled Cardiff Architecture: A Comparison of Traditional and Contemporary Design in which I compare and contrast the shift in architectural design over time in the Welsh capital.

In Interactive New Media I created an informative ‘web app’ about the solar system designed for tablets and desktop computers called Solar. Users are presented with an experience that is highly visual and makes heavy use of animation to display facts and information in a way that is fun and engaging to use and explore.
