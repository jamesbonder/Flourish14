About Jack's Work

This is my independent study for my final year in University. This is a concept design for an application for the University of South Wales and makes use of the UniLife features but in a mobile friendly format.

I want to go into UI/UX design for mobile/tablet devices in the future, but for the time being I am going to America & Canada to work in a summer camp & travel.

I have thoroughly enjoyed my three years in the University of South Wales and would like to thank all lecturers & staff for aiding me in the journey.
