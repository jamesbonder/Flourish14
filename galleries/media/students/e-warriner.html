Elizabeth Warriner

BA (Hons) Media Production


About Elizabeth's Work

I am a 21 year old student who has just finished a Media Production degree at the University of South Wales. I have majored in Film and Video, specifically, the genre of Documentary as I love finding ways to show people representations within society and the world in an engaging way.

To me, Documentary lets people see a part of life that they otherwise possibly wouldn't have and it opens people's eyes to new possibilities. Editing has always been my favourite part of post-production although recently I have found a passion for producing after trying out the role as part of a competition.

I am originally from Devon but am going move away so that I can further my aspects for the future and have access to a wide variety of exciting roles in the industry. I also love street photography which I have been able to progress in at Uni. It has enabled me to become more confident in approaching strangers for tasks. I am happy to have finished my degree but nervous and excited about what the future holds. I am looking to further my skills and use my knowledge to create wonderful things in the future.
