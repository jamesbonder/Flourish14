About Natalie's Work

I've been playing and writing music for 8 years now and have worked with a variety of different musicians to create a variety of music. I'm primarily a bass guitarist, have started playing at 14 years of age, but soon became interested in song-writing as well as band work. Currently I have been working on a number of solo and collaboratative projects involving Popular Music, Music Tech, Sound Tech, Visual FX and Motion Graphics and Animation students from USW.

The two modules, Songwriting and Composition For Media have encouraged me to work with others, fine-tuning my creative process and allowing me to explore different areas of composition and production, using Logic Pro X. I'm incredibly hard working and dedicated, as music is something that has been my passion for many years. The work I've showcased here demonstrates my skills as a composer, performer, producer and collaborater.
