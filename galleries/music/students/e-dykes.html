Elliot Dykes

BA (Hons) Popular Music

About Elliot's Work

I'm a composer and sound designer who plays a number of instruments including 13 years in guitar. I have a passion in writing new music and being creative in editing sounds, as well as studying new genres and cultures to help me understand more about the kind of music they play and how I could incorporate it into my own writing.

Writing for media is a passion project that I would like to continue as a career as soon as I can. Being able to put music onto a screen, or into a video game or on a radio is something that pushes me forward into trying something different and finding new ways to approach a problem. I've been able to use this when collaborating with students from both film and animation students this year.

The university has taught me to discover who I am as a musician, and as a person. I've been able to express myself musically, and been able to make contacts with other creative people to hopefully work on future projects together.
