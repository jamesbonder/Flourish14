About David's Work

Hello, my name is David Harrison, soon to be a 23 year old BA Performance & Media Graduate from Swindon. I am very interested in creating comedy short films and I soon hope to be studying a masters in writing for comedy. Coming from a background in musical theatre (and having to change my future plans due to injury), the Performance and Media course offered me a range of different media modules and opportunities to explore and fall in love with. Over the past 3 years I have made short films, presented live TV, created radio dramas, performed in mixed media theatre pieces and collaborated with other modules to create work.

My main goal when devising projects is to entertain, so please check out some of my projects created in the past three years and enjoy! And if you happen to be a fan of rubbish urban gangs, disaster dinner parties, romantic knitwear and cliche American sitcoms - you're in for a treat!

SEEKER from David Harrison on Vimeo.

What if video footage of someone you believed to be dead, changed?

Solo Final Major Project: Film and Video

When a new law states that everyone over the age of 18 has to record a 3 minute "last words/will" video before they die , Izzie becomes obsessed with the video left to her by her late partner (TOM) who dies whilst fighting for his country in a war overseas. When the video starts to take over her life, one day the message changes and a whole new message is played, with Tom asking for help and th at people are watching her.

The Lion in Love from David Harrison on Vimeo.

A ridiculous take on British Gang films. Based on the fable "The Lion in Love". A surreal, comedy, with the look and feel of a child’s imagination mixed with a really uncool brit-pop music video. Two rival gangs, a trap and unrequited love.
