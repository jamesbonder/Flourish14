Amy Jacks

BA (Hons) Performance and Media

About Amy's Work

I'm Amy. I have a passion for creating film that is different and 'outside the box'.

I have had experience creating promotional films for Arriva Trains Wales, BAFTA and a multi-platform event called Transformatlab. I am eager to continue to work within the film industry and I am currently working on ideas to turn into short films to then enter into competitions and festivals.

I also have a passion for writing scripts that make you think and a lot of the time have shocking twists.

My degree has strengthened my skills in all aspects of the industry from ideas to post production and has increased my determination to pursue a career in this field of work.

Please look around my page and I hope you enjoy my work.
