Charlotte John

BA (Hons) Performance and Media

About Charlotte's Work

I have always been a storyteller. From being three years old and putting on plays for my family using my toys as my castmates, to writing numerous short stories as a child, through to my A Level in Theatre Studies, storytelling has been a constant in my life.

My degree in Performance and Media has allowed me to experience various methods and disciplines involved in storytelling, including performance for both stage and screen, as well as writing, directing, and editing films. During my time at the University of South Wales, I have discovered that my passion and strengths lie in scriptwriting, which I hope to pursue as a career. However, I am interested in all aspects of filmmaking, especially editing and camera operating, and I have enjoyed acting as a camera operator on a number of my peers' films.

I have learnt a lot about myself as a filmmaker during my degree, and I will continue to make films after I graduate. This page contains a few of the films I have produced during my third year, and more of my work can be found at charlotteajohn.wordpress.com
