Rachael Anne Roberts

BA (Hons) Performance and Media

About Rachael's Work

Hello there! My name is Rachael, I am 21.

My main talents are presenting, writing and performing and I about to graduate with a degree in Performance and Media where I have learnt the skill of filming, recording and editing video and sound. This year I was Station Manager of my universities radio station Tequila Radio and I know well how to lead a team, as well as be an active part of one.

I have 6 years experience in radio presenting and have experience as an online presenter such as the internet game Pixel Picnics and I have worked with Somewhereto_ as a presenter, as well as being a presenter for Exposure TV! In terms of acting, I have had the lead in two small budget films, horror 'Hollow' and a romantic drama, and smaller parts in other productions.

I am a very determined, enthusiastic person and handle pressure very well. I enjoy sports such as karate, cheerleading and swimming, (in my mermaids tail of course!) I hope my work entertains you and feel free to check out my website where you will find my YouTube channel and more !
