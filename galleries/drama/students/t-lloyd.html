About Tristan's Work

My name is Tristan. I am a fluent Welsh-speaker originally from west Wales. My main interests lie in acting (both screen and theatre) and presenting. University has given me a great insight into semi-professional environments related to the media industry, particularly after being involved in presenting for Exposure TV.

Outside of education I am a member of various acting agencies and a bilingual street-acting production company based in Cardiff, which have given me invaluable experiences in professional surroundings, including Welsh soap opera 'Pobol y Cwm', Doctor Who and various radio commercials.

Now that I own my own filming equipment I aim to continue creating short films to improve and showcase my skills in cinematography and editing, two fields that I also share a great interest in.

On this page I have included my major project for my final year, 'Any Last Words', along with a presenter trail and showreel of my presenting skills during my time on Exposure TV.

Diolch/Thank you.
