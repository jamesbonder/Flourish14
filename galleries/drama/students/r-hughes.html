Rachel Hughes

BA (Hons) Performance and Media

About Rachel's Work

I am a third year student with a major interest in being a radio presenter. BUT because of the nature of my course I have put my finger in many pies as it were. I always try to use my personal feelings/experiences in my pieces because they are raw, exposing yourself in a performance and giving it everything you have, for me, is the nature of performance - and it does make for interesting work.

Through my youtube channel you can see a variety of pieces I have either acted in or else written and directed, same with my soundcloud profile - there are a few scapes and examples of my presenting for Exposure radio.

I am a girl from Belfast who can speak two languages and who enjoys trying something new, either shadow work or physical theatre, or new effects to convey emotion, I like to try every which way to make a piece work, and I find it is the best way because the first idea isn't the only idea, right?
