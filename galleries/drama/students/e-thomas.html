Ellen Thomas

BA (Hons) Performance and Media

About Ellen' Work

My name is Ellen and I have completed a degree in BA Performance and Media. Having always excelled in creativity, I decided to take this course to explore the vast amount of outlets available to me for a future career in the arts; for example screen acting, film production, and TV presenting. Over my time studying here in Cardiff, I have enjoyed these three aspects the most and hope to develop my skills in these areas for some time to come.

My other interests include animals and wildlife, history, music and politics, and believe my time studying this course has allowed me to express my love of these interests through various mediums. I feel I have developed into a well-rounded, confident person, and I pride myself on my glass-half-full approach to work... and life in general!
