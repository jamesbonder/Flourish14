About Amy's Work

Hello! My name is Amy Mills, I’m 20 years old and I have just finished my final year on the Performance and Media course at the University of South Wales. I’m an aspiring tv/radio presenter, with a keen interest in television production. Ever since I can remember I have always been fascinated by the world of media, and remember watching the tv thinking “I want to do that!”. To think that now in the early stages of my career I am starting to make it happen is amazing.

In my final year at university, I was contacted by a producer from Media Trust, based on London, and asked whether I would be able to form a production team and create, and present, our own episode as part of a tv series called Brilliant Britain. We have created a special for that series (Brilliant Wales) which aired in March 2014, and are just finishing our own local episode, based in Cardiff, that will hopefully be broadcast in the coming months.

Also this year I was approached to become a News Reporter on Radio Cardiff. I’ve been doing the afternoon news for the station twice a week since November 2013, and I'm now the stand in presenter and traffic reporter for the Monday Breakfast show as well.

My time at university has definitely strengthened my love of the chaotic but extremely rewarding industry that is broadcasting, and I hope that it will help me build myself a long and successful career.
