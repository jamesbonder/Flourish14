About Dan Y Wenallt / Under Milkwood

Wrth i ni ddathlu 100 mlwyddiant Dylan Thomas eleni roedd dewis cynhyrchu Dan y Wenallt yn beth amlwg i’w wneud efallai, ond trwy gymryd addasiad gwych T James Jones ynghyd a’r gwreiddiol fel sail i broses greadigol, fe rydym ni fel cwmni, wedi darganfod ac yn gallu dathlu rhywbeth arall hefyd, sef ein bod, o gadernid ein hunaniaeth fel Cymry Cymraeg (o pob math o gefndiroedd), yn falch ac yn freintiedig i allu dewis cyfathrebu yn ddwyieithog weithiau.

Celebrating Dylan Thomas’s centenary this year, a production of Under Milk Wood may have been an obvious choice, but by taking T James Jones’s beautiful adaptation as well as the original as the basis for a creative process, we as a company have discovered and are able to celebrate something else too, which is that secure in our identity as Welsh speakers (from all sorts of backgrounds) we are privileged to be able to choose to communicate bilingually sometimes.
