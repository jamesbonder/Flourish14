About Alessandra's Work

Hello there!, my name is Alessandra (Ally to my friends). I have recently finished my 3rd year at university, studying 2D animation, and I'm currently adding the final touches to my major film, "There be Monsters" which you can see in my showreel.

I have a deep passion for creating, whether it be designing new characters or giving them life through animation. I have a rather wild, overactive imagination, and inventing characters who are bizarre and quirky, then developing a personality in them through their movement and voice, it's a real joy to do. It's impossible to stop me once I get started on a new project, and I'll go through each new challenge with optimism and boundless energy.

My time at the University has proven that I can accomplish so much with determination, a positive attitude, and a little bit of madness! It has helped me to explore the numerous approaches and techniques used in the medium. You can take so many different paths with this course and broaden your skills in all stages of the animation process. From Script to screen, and all are equally fulfilling to do.

I now intend to pursue my career in character animation/design, further honing my skills and reaching for the first rung on the job ladder. This is an industry that I adore, I love what it can produce and all the people behind it, and to be a part of such a lively bunch is an incredible thing.
