About Liz's Work

Hi there! My name is Liz and I'm a freelance visual development artist currently living in Cardiff. I have been drawing for as long as I can remember, and its something which I never really grew out of!

I specialise in Conceptual art for Animation, Film, Game and Illustration.

I take keen interest in character design, illustration and storyboarding and I am always seeking out new methods and opportunities to further expand upon my drawing skills. In my spare time, I can often be found doodling in cafes accompanied by a hot chocolate, playing video games or catching up on my backlog of comic books.

Feel free to check out my blog if you would like to see more of my work!
