About Felix's Work

I have just finished my third year of BA Computer Animation at the University of South Wales. I have had some experience in the industry through work experiences at 'A Productions' in Bristol and 'Black Rock Studio' is Brighton.

My specialties involve animation, rendering, rigging and dynamic simulations. This year I was the animator on "Pathfinder" which is a VFX sci-fi short, rigged a dragon with dynamic properties for a CG short called "Time freeze" and I also directed "Till Death Do Us Part" which is a virtual reality game using the oculus rift as a platform.

I am a talented, intelligent, focused technical designer with a yearning to increase my knowledge.
