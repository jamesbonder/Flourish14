Rebecca Rootes

BA (Hons) Computer Animation


About Rebecca's Work

Hi, I have just completed my final year in Computer Animation, specializing as an artist. I particularly enjoyed learning ZBrush software for digital sculpting in conjunction with Maya to create detailed models for animation.

I have gained valuable experience working with others from my involvement in collaborative projects, including 'The Awoken', a VFX short featuring a predatory creature that I modelled and textured and a ten second sting, featuring an enthusiastic fawn that I also designed.

I now plan to further my sculpting skills in ZBrush and Photoshop through online courses and hope to gain employment in the animation industry in the near future.

View more of Rebecca's work at her website.
