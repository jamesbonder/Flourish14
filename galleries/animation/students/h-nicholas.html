About Heloise's Work

I'm a 3rd year student and I've just finished studying a Animation BA(Hone), specialising in concept art.

Through 3 years of study my styles and interests have been pushed in to a different direction, it is now my wish to specialise in fashion and erotic illustration, both in traditional and digital media. I want to further my skills in after effects and photoshop in pursuit of mastering my own style of 2.5D imagery. I am also continuing to develop my skills in traditional media, including gouache, watercolour, ink and air brushing.

I hope that in the future I am able to make my own mark in the world of Pin Up art and unique Illustrations. I want to be able to further the message on the power of women's sensuality, that it is not sexist or demeaning to take pride in dressing as feminine as you want. Women has the power, they just need to embrace it any way they can.

I am currently working on a series of sensual illustrations I am hoping to use as my debut as a professional artist. I'm looking forward to a career I can take pride in and that feeds my passion.
