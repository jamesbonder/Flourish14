Megan Watkins

BA (Hons) Computer Animation


About Megan's Work

My name is Megan Watkins and I am a third year Computer Animation student at the Atrium, Cardiff. I specialise in 3D modelling, and although character modelling was always my initial focus, I have recently gained a keen interest in hard service modelling also.

I recently finished working on my major project for my final year where I modelled three characters, But I also focused one project purely on props and an environment to show a range of abilities across subject. My passion lies with anatomy, and my dissertation was based purely on the importance of anatomy for CG artists.

I also directed a project which is a medical video diagram about the causes of a heart attack. The reason I have created this project is gain a greater insight into human anatomy and medical conditions, and from this I will carry on this work after university and hopefully obtain the opportunity to work in a CG medical animation company.
