About Emma's Work

Hello my name is Emma Garrod.

I am 21 years old originally from Newcastle Upon Tyne

I have just finished a three year course in animation. Studying at the University of South Wales in Cardiff. please check out my blog for my current activities.

I specialise in Conceptual art for Animation, Film, Game and Illustration.

Later this year I will begin a Masters Degree in Visual Development at The Academy of Art in San Francisco!

I love drawing, films, coffee shop doodles and Pixar ...

My other hobbies apart from drawing are:- travelling, going to concerts, films, reading, cooking, meeting new people, socialising, climbing, the outdoors and walking my dog Scuttle.
