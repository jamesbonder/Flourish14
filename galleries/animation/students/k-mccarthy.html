About Kim's Work

My name is Kim McCarthy, I am 22 and from Cardiff, South Wales.

I am a CG / 3D artist having just completed my third year in BA Computer Animation at the ATRiuM University of South Wales. I enjoy all aspects of CG art production, specialising in character and environment modelling.

Primarily I work with Autodesk Maya, zBrush, Mudboz and Adobe Photoshop & Premiere. I am also passionate about digital paingting working with a tablet or cintiq. With a background in illustration I enjoy and am both confident working in both.
