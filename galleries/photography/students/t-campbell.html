About Tom's Work

NURTURING NATURE: Protecting the Natural Balance Tom Campbell

The work here looks at the world of conservation within the UK to document the great effort that is constantly happening to protect our natural world. The images explore the death of wildlife by human hands as well as the new life that exists only through the help of conservationists.

This project documents the working lives of conservationists in a variety of different forms from small volunteer led charities to larger membership funded organisations. It takes on a documentary style as well as including classic wildlife and landscape imagery.

This is a photographic story of the wild lives that are too often overlooked and the heroic individuals who are constantly battling to restore a balance in the natural ecosystem by Nurturing Nature.

“In every walk with Nature one receives far more than he seeks.” -John Muir
