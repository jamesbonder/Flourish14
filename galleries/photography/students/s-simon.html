Sophie Simon

BA (Hons) Photography


About Sophie's Work

M A T T E R / F A C T.

Since its invention, the photograph has been a means in which society have perceived their own realities, noting the family photograph in particular. The family photograph, or family album, is something a familial unit once cherished. The family album is something that is assumed to be integral to our past and our sense of family through history a sense of ‘shared’ history.

What if you do not feel sentiment about memories before your time?

I came into possession of a tin of photographs, a tin that depicted the majority of my grandmother’s youth (also documenting other family members), up to the time my mother, uncle and aunt were born. I felt no connection to the photographs, no nostalgic sentiment, Those depicted in the images felt like strangers; familiar strangers.

This project is about creating a connection between myself and these photographs, establishing a relationship to memories that existed before my own existence.
