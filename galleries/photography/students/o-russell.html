Olivia Russell

BA (Hons) Photography


About Olivia's Work

I am aspiring photographer, who is due to graduate this summer. I will be looking to further my career in my chosen field of photography. I enjoy all aspects of photography but have a passion for landscape photography. I love landscape photography because it allows me to get back to nature and capture the natural beauty of the world that so many of us seem to take for granted or just miss in todays busy society.

I enjoy both colour and black and white photography and feel that the last three years training has proved invaluable to my development and understanding of photography not only as an art but also as a technical process. The projects that were assigned have allowed me to gain varied experience in all aspects of photographic practice from portraiture to commercial and many more. I have set up a Facebook page and a website using Weebly. I am currently available to undertake any opportunities presented to me.
