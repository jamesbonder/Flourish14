Chelsea Edwards

BA (Hons) Photography


About Chelsea's Work

My name is Chelsea Edwards, and I have just completed my final year in BA Photography at the Atrium at the University of South Wales. Photography has always been my passion and continuous hobby since a very young age. I spend my Summers at Gower, an area of outstanding beauty in Swansea, and here is where I find my inspiration for photography, be in landscape or photographing Natural Wildlife, or the odd surf shot here and there. I enjoy every moment of it.

My Photography Degree allowed me to explore aspects which I might not have first considered. The theoretical aspects of the degree opened my eyes to the way in which people read photographs, or how they are used. This is a key factor to consider when producing photographers, and I wouldn't have even thought about it if it was part of my course.

I will continue to work within my passion, even when I leave the University of South Wales. I am hoping to continue my work on film photography, as this is something I thoroughly enjoyed in my three years.
