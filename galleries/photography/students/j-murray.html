Jamie E. Murray

BA (Hons) Documentary Photography


About Jamie's Work

Jamie E. Murray is a photographer based between South Wales and London. Currently studying Documentary Photography at the University of South Wales, Newport, his work centres on the resonance and relevance of trends and cultures within modern society.

This includes a current long term project he is undertaking that looks into youth culture through focusing on individual stories and livelihoods which in turn can portray the bigger picture. He tries to maintain this ethic of using personal stories to represent larger issues in his other work including commissions and side projects.

View more of Jamie's work at his website.
