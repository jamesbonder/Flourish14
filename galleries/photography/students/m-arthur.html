About Mitchell's Work

After studying Photography for the past three years, my final work focuses around the use of hallucinogenic drugs titled “External High”. Below is the text that accompanies my work.

The stigma behind illegal drugs and legal highs are always the topic of discussion within the news, how musicians have died of an overdose or the debate behind the class system of drugs. Taking a drug from each class, LSD from class A, Cannabis from class B, and Ketamine from class C, three drugs which can all cause hallucinogenic effects of the user, External High is a project that questions our perception behind these drugs.

By looking at the form of projection, External high provides three films that discuss first hand experiences with each drug, as each subject provides their own thoughts on the topics of religion, medicinal purposes and recreational use all within a film that aims to represent the experience that occurs inside their mind on the outside of their body.
