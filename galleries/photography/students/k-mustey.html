Karl Mustey

About Karl's Work

I am a photographer with an added passion for architecture and design. I have produced this body of work for my major project as part of an ongoing body of work that documents my favourite location, the coast.

On the Verge of Change: The Transition of Littoral South Wales is an extensive documentary project I have produced to obtain a visual reference of the coastal regions of South Wales, ranging from Aberavon in the west to the City of Cardiff in the east. There has been a considerable change in the use of the land in this area, from the shipping of coal and steel in the times of heavy industry to the leisure industries and more recent housing developments, a constant metamorphosis continues along littoral South Wales. The reclamation and regeneration of the landscape and architecture offers a unique visual subject matter, this is what draws me to the area and to photograph it.

The work has been shot in two main styles; at night, using long exposures and the ambient illuminations of the developed areas; and from a topographical viewpoint using wide angles, often perpendicular to the subject matter. The images follow three themes: leisure, industry and housing, and these themes are repeated throughout.
