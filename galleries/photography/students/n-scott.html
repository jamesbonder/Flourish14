About Naomi's Work

It has always been a desire of mine to do something child orientated and I have found my niche in children's photography.The natural, most likely ‘ignored’ daily moments, are what I strive on. These captured moments are parts of the child’s vulnerable growth. Moments to cherish forever which cannot be replicated by a ‘posed’ set-up; they are valued due to their worth from a click on the shutter- paused moment in time, never to be seen exactly alike again.

My work features young children being natural within the everyday obstacles and interests to that age group. It’s based on how they make sense of the world around them, and how they then tackle that by interaction. Photographing children requires much patience, as it’s easy to stray away from the main focus with their large personalities, and short attention spans.

“It’s the innocence, expressions, and character of children, whilst caught at the right moment that really makes the time and energy spent photographing a child, worthwhile.”
