About Alaoighse's Work

Alaoighse O’Hanrahan is a photographer who studies the unique qualities of the human form and individual natures of each person within the frame.

The most recent series of work titled ‘innocence’ focuses on the universal delicateness connected with childhood. The series of intimate photographs highlight the openness and fragility of children. Each image hosts a variety of backdrops surrounding the protagonist of the work. The young child within the frame provokes an emotionally reminiscent reaction from the viewer as she slowly welcomes us into her world one photograph at a time.

View more of Alaoighse's work at her website.
