About Rhian's Work

'Memory'

There is you and there is me. We find ourselves in the pages of family albums passed, within the flickering images of that antique camcorder, and we hold onto these recollections within our minds. There either is or isn't a truth to what we recall and feel. That freckle on your cheek. That tear within your eye. That wistful expression staring back at you. The shades of memory are determined by you and me...for whatever we lose within ourselves, we recreate within our minds. We do not recall what occurred, but reminisce upon what we choose to retain. This is a story about memory; it's beauty, it's confusion, and it's destruction. What you make of it is yours...and yours, alone.

My final major project was based around the concept of memory. To evoke a sense of nostalgia or to make a viewer contemplate on a past moment, even for just a minute, is what I planned for my series- to highlight the power of memories.

Within my photographic work I like to create a variety of fashion editorial and advertorial portraits that possess an element of narration to engage with the viewers.
