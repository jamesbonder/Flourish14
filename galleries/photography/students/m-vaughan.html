Meg Vaughan

BA (Hons) Photographic Art


About Meg's Work

I am an art photography graduate looking forward to a career in the education sector. I have a strong passion for a variety of different aspects of art - from analogue and digital photography to fine art painting and sculptural pieces. These interests have been developed throughout University study, from a diversity of proposals and through the development of my own personal projects.

I attempt every artistic project with enthusiasm and a professional attitude, ensuring work is mature and exciting to the audience. Seeing the world through a camera lens allows me to express my creativity. It justly reflects my interest in art and photography; leading to me wanting to teach others as passionate as me.
