Joe Loveridge

About Joe's Work

Meal Of Man –

An artistic photographic portrayal on how modern society relates the intimate female body to food, using slang that incorporates food I took literal understandings of these and created a series of still life images that incorporate vivid colours to reflect the implicit sexual understandings of these images. These beliefs come from the development of “Laddish” behaviour in todays’ world and by extension the understandings of representation in the differences of genders that occur throughout the world due to how men often refer and idolise the female form.

Street –

A commercial attempt at how people are seen by the world and how they see themselves. A belief that is incorporated by High Street brands, utilizing this to target their markets, as Fashion is seen as an ideology and an idea of self-identity. I collected examples of how individuals differ from each other through visual imagery, specifically choosing people that I felt stood out from the crowd and understood how to effectively create a separate identity through mass produced clothing. This work is produced into a booklet, as a recreation of what would be found as part of a fashion brand or apparel store.
