About Amethys' Work

What does the colour red represent to you? Anger and romance? Two polar opposite ends of the emotion scale yet so easily linked together by one colour

Human interaction with the colour red creates this immediate seas of emotion when the colour is mentioned or seen. Initially, I disliked the colour red. I did not like that it represents two opposing emotions. To me, that seemed a little confusing and contradictory. But now, i'm not so sure. Romance - that nice, warm feeling, roses and hears but can so easily lead to disappointment, heartbreak, anger, frustration. It makes more sense than I first thought.
