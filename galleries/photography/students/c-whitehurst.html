About Charlotte's Work

I am a graduate photographer who specialises in hair and beauty photography. Hair and beauty has always been a passion of mine since a young age and it's only within the 3 years of studying at university that this passion has shown throughout my photography.

I have a range of different skills within the studio and lighting. I use these skills to get the best out of my photos. I always strive to create interesting images using different lighting techniques. I also have an interest in fashion and portrait photography.
