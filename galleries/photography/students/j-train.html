About Joanna's Work

Climate Change is the greatest threat facing humanity but many people feel that not enough is being done to prevent it. Many governments seem reluctant to put in place laws necessary to prevent climate change but equally, the general public have been slow to adopt a ‘greener’ lifestyle. My work asks whether advertising can be used to influence people’s behaviour so that they live more sustainable lifestyles?

My advertising campaign aims to confront people with the consequences of their consumerist behaviour and the impact it will have on future generations. The tag-line, ‘Buy Now, Your Children will Pay Later’, has clear associations with credit, which has made the consumer society possible, but sooner or later debt always has to be repaid.

Appealing photographs of children, innocently playing with toys, draw in the audience, only to realise that each toy relates directly to an area of consumerism that is having a negative impact upon the planet. Worst of all, it is the child who will suffer if people are not prepared to change their behaviour.

‘Credit cards’ linked to the posters, provide more information about the harm caused, but also highlight the website, 'Save the future'. I have created with positive suggestions for environmentally friendly living.
