About Lucia's Work

My final major project titled Time Zones explores the sense of disorientation that a person feels when experiencing jet lag. The images in the series have been produced at night in various locations on The East Coast of America and South Wales, United Kingdom, although in many of the images the viewer is unable to distinguish the images origin.
