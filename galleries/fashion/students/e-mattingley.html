About Emma's Work

I am an aspiring illustrator and soon to be a graduate of the University of South Wales, with a BA Hons in Fashion Promotion. My work has been described or related to pop art and street art. I am hugely influenced by Kitsch Cartoons, 90s fashion, anything pastel, food (mostly fast), old school tattoo art, Pinterest, and Tumblr.
