About Harrriette's Work

The main purpose for my final major project is to establish and brand myself as a fashion stylist and create an online promotional strategy through social media and a fashion blog. I have created an online portfolio that contains eight photo shoot’s which shows a variety of my styling and art direction. My style is fairly simple, where I aim to balance colour and textures so the look isn't too much. I've also made sure that my self-branding reflects my personality and styling by keeping it neat and classic.

My fashion blog Kirsty Flora Jones is the perfect way to showcase my personal style and ideas with the world. I plan to make my brand a success by being consistent with my fashion blog as it’s the main source of promotion. The blog contains frequent updates of fashion news and personal style, which is linked and promoted through social media.

Along with my Fashion Promotion degree I have made a start in the fashion industry by assisting fashion stylists Alexis Knox and Alex Longmore on magazine editorials, advertising campaigns and celebrities. This has given me great insight into the busy and exciting industry and how fashion stylist’s work.
