About Jess' Work

Jessica Campbell-Ploverâ€™s ethos as a designer is fulfilling fantasies, bathing in glamour and creating show girl alter egos. Seductive and feminine with a hint of creepy glamour, her clothing allows the viewer to form characters with the clothing. Excited by vintage pieces, each garment has a nod to eras gone by but with a quirky twist. Her aim is all about taking inspiration from the past and letting people access glamour whilst being creative and experimental with what they wear and allow them to transform into someone else through clothing.

The Dainty Dotty collection resembles a day at the fun fair along Blackpool promenade, basking in kitsch galore, and then enjoying the mystique of the circus in the night time, surrounded by glimmering lights and enchanting characters. Imagine that the show girl and the clown combine to create a look that pairs lingerie pieces with voluminous, playful shapes, candy bright colours, sumptuous fabric s and luxurious embellishments. Specifically, inspired by 1920s-1940s circus and burlesque imagery, the designer invites you to experience her glittering world and be swept away with the story that her final collection portrays.
