About Abby's Work

I am a fashion promotion student graduating in 2014, when leaving university I am looking for a position within a marketing agency as that is where my main skills lie and I wish to utilise them the best I can within a subject that I feel passion for.

Marketing is one of the key elements that make a brand successful and the best way to engage communication to their customer.I therefore decided to create a 12 month marketing strategy for the country lifestyle brand John Partridge as my final major project at university. Established in 1969, they aim themselves to thirty to forty-year-old men and women. Choosing a brand that isn't directed straight to my age group allows me to develop my skills of being able to market anything to anyone. I also constructed a brand analysis for John Partridge showing my skills in reading a business and its strengths and weaknesses.
