About Jade 's Work

An aspiring stylist, Jade has taken part in many photo-shoots, dressing, planning and photographing, this also includes the organisation of photo-shoots for the yearly university fashion magazine Platfform 13. Having a keen interest in costume as well Jade has assisted on the feature film 'Knights of the Dead' in 2012. With a passion for not only styling Jade has a keen interest in trend forecasting with an intrigue for colours and silhouettes.

Personal style and stories are the inspiration for the Final Major Project that has recently been completed, Style Atlas is a new concept of website which consists of her own street photography and short videos. It is a project which portrays a new perspective of Cardiff in a personal way representing the locals in their natural habitat, fashionable locals, locals' favourite places in Cardiff and small businesses that should have more recognition.

This website will hopefully develop into more cities in the UK contrasting the findings and offering people to see a city through her own personal eyes and to witness the natural style and personality of the local inhabitants.
