Ellie Louise Gomery

BA (Hons) Fashion Promotion


About Ellie 's Work

I have recently completed the BA (Hons) Fashion Promotion Course, which has helped me to specialize in the fashion buying and merchandising industry sector. This is the tailored career path I wish to pursue. My summer plans involve additional specialist study in fashion buying and merchandising, luxury fashion brand management and product design both in London and Dubai. I am also interning in the luxury fashion industry for two months in Shanghai, China. My overall goal is to work as a fashion buyer or merchandiser within the fashion industry.

Throughout my Final Major Project, which accounted for 60 credits of my final degree, I acted as a freelance brand and market consultant for reflective fashion brand, LFLECT. I aimed to reposition LFLECT in the market by presenting the knitwear collection in a fashionable way and creating a new vision for the brand.

Other aims included creating a brand strategy to help increase sales, and raise brand awareness through market analysis and strategic planning. I directed, styled, photographed and filmed the creative outcomes of my project. These were a look book showing the functional and fashionable product elements, branded business cards, a fashion film, and in-store promotional material such as swing tags, and a poster. Using augmented reality (LAYAR) as a digital platform; it connects all the creative elements, allowing viewers to scan the promotional outcomes taking them to a website which hosts digital look book and the fashion film, showing the products movement and wear ability.
