About Jennifer's Work

Sense the Opportunity

Born out of my desire to pursue a career in fashion buying my final major project explores the power of the senses in fashion buying. rstand the brand as well as know its current market, brand positioning, USP, competitors and other factors to the brand.

For the project I created a multi-sensory exhibition guide which was created for Pure London, a highly reputable trade show that exhibited a variety of international fashion brands. This new multi-sensory exhibition guide was created in answer to the current unappealing show guide available at the Spring Summer 14 Pure London exhibition. The multi-sensory guide aims to offer the reader a unique brand experience, encouraging the reader to taste, touch, see, hear and smell the exhibiting fashion brands. The senses are showcased in a highly visual, professional exhibition guide that would be made available to fashion buyers visiting the show.

TThe senses are so powerful yet underutilised in the fashion industry. The Pure London multi-sensory exhibition guide aims to use the power of the senses to showcase new brands in a totally unique manner. The guide also makes traditional maps and trade show information interactive and engaging to a wide audience.

This truly unique sensory guide is the first of its kind and encourages buyers to “sense the opportunity” of carefully selected fashion brands.
