About Kara's Work

After completing the Work Placement diploma in year two, my final year of study has been directed towards the corporate and social responsibility of fast fashion retailers.

My experience in buying has encouraged me to critically analyze supply chain structure and innovate new or additional processes that could pro-actively benefit the ethical responsibility of a retailer.

Researching for my dissertation allowed me to strategize potential theories that could influence the ethical practice of a fashion brand. A few areas I have researched include: consumer consumption & psychology, fa√ßade ethical branding and the importance of transparency along the supply chain.

I am most interested in educating brands and their customers about the importance of ethical trade by sharing my knowledge of fashion systems so they are able to make educated and conscious fashion purchases.

The Annual Penny Bazaar has been designed as an extension of Marks & Spencer's Plan A strategy, it aims to integrate the sustainable values of the company further than just the high street and act as a service to the British public by creating community activity and encouraging eco-friendly attitudes towards consumerism.
