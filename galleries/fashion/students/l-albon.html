Lewis Albon

BA (Hons) Fashion Promotion


About Lewis' Work

I am a passionate and hard working graduate of BA (HONS) Fashion Promotion at the University of South Wales. I have worked hard at university to achieve good grades as well as being an active member within the university by being the course representative for all 3 years, chair of the student council and part of the executive committee.

I have won awards for my contribution to the students union and the Platfform showcases as well as winning the CCI Faculty Course representative of the year award for my devotion to improve the course and the university.

I am a professional and approachable person and I always try to push myself to do more and to do better.

During my studies I have been involved in many group projects with my peers such as opening a pop-up-shop in Cardiff city centre and working on modules such as Brand Promotion, Colour & Trend Forecasting, Fashion Illustration, Promotional Campaign Development, Fashion Media and Fashion Photography. I have worked on live briefs for brands including Ted Baker, Urban Outfitters and Swarovski.

I have also organised a fashion show for first year design students and co-organised and executed two fashion galas for graduating third year students.

I am skilled at working digitally on software such as Adobe Photoshop and InDesign to develop graphics, images and branding as well as photo editing. My main areas of work are in technical design and event management/co-ordination.
