Emily Franks

BA (Hons) Fashion Design


About Emily's Work

Recent fashion design graduate of University of South Wales, Emily Franks has created the Science of Fashion brand with a view to change perception about what fashion can be.

Inspired hugely by maths and science, her first collection Flux Frontier is an embodiment of a positive future for fashion.

Attempting to broadening horizons through an innovate approach to sustainable fashion.

With the belief that Sustainability and ethics are the key to true quality.

View more of Emily's work at her website.
