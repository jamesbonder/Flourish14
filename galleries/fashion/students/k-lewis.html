Kathryn Lewis

BA (Hons) Fashion Design


About Kathryn's Work

I am fashion designer with a unique and innovative approach to creating unisex collections.I have become recongnised by my conceptual and unique style. My ‘nomadic’ ethos continually influencesevery aspect of her design and development process.

My collection is defined by distinctive aesthetic, meticulous detail, considered functionality and sustainable intelligence. My experimental work has also brought sustainable design to a main- stream fashion audience and presents a new definition of luxury with integrity.

The refined attention to detail and thought provoking narrative give my collections both ingenuity and great longevity, both a true reflection of the commitment and dedication of how I work as a designer
