Katherine Mundy

BA (Hons) Photography


About Katherine's Work

In this project I have combined my two passions photography and textiles. I really loved the idea of combining images with material which is an aspect that my project has explored.

'Memories From A Distance’ seeks to explore the combination of old family photos with images of landscapes and places to which I have a personal connection. For me it’s a journey into the past to discover my family history and to re-tell it. My aim is to create a sense of familiarity within the images, by removing these people from their original photographs and by constructing a new narrative which I can relate to.
